The SAP Calculator is an analysis tool that allows you to easily calculate the approximate odds of your team beating another team. The SAP Calculator simulates the defined battle 1000 times and determines the percentage of wins, draws and loses.

Disclaimer: The SAP Calculator is not an official tool of the game and is not affiliated with the game's developers. The SAP Calculator is a fan-made tool, and is not intended to replace the official game.

The SAP Calculator engine is based on the official game engine, but it may not be 100% accurate. It is pretty darn close though, and should be completely accurate for nearly every possible battle.

Guide

Select the pack that the player and opponent are using. This determines the pets that are available to each player.

Toy configuration.

  • Angler Mode: The opposite player's pets are available to be selected for each player. (This does not affect pets that summon from your pack pet pool)
  • All Pets: All pets are availble to select. (This does not affect pets that summon from your pack pet pool)
  • Old Stork: Stork summons pets with base stats.
  • Token Pets: Token pets are available to select. (This does not affect pets that summon from your pack pet pool)
  • Komodo Shuffle: Shuffle pet order each battle in front of a Komondo.
  • Mana: Show/hide mana stat on the pet selector.

Adjust the zoom level of the application.

Toggle advanced settings.

Advanced settings. They allow you to configure the battle to a more specific scenario.

  • Turn: The turn the battle takes place. This will affect pets such as the Stork, Eagle, and Stegosaurus.
  • Player Gold Spent: Player gold spent the previous round. This will affect the Meerkat.
  • Opponent Gold Spent: Same as above but for the Opponent player.
  • Player Roll Amount: The amount of times the Player rolled in the shop phase.
  • Opponent Roll Amount: Same as above but for the Opponent player.
  • Player Lvl 3 Sold: The amount of times the Player sold a Level 3 pet.
  • Opponent Lvl 3 Sold: Same as above but for the Opponent player.
  • Player Summoned Amt: The amount of times the Player summoned a pet in the shop phase.
  • Opponent Summoned Amt: Same as above but for the Opponent player.

This is the board. The left side is the Player pets and the right side is the Opponent Pets. Each white box contains a pet and its' stats/equipment. The layout of the pets are identical to as they would be in battle. The right most Player pet is clashing with the left most Opponent pet.

This is a pet.

  • A: This select box is where you may choose the pet that will occupy this space.
  • B: This select box is where you may choose the pet's equipment.
  • C: These fields are where you may input the pet's stats. The gray field is attack, the red field is health, and the indigo field is mana.
  • D: This component determines the amount of exp the pet has. A pet may have 0-5 exp denoted by the number of boxes highlighted. The numbers (1, 2, 3) on top of the exp bar signify the level of the pet. 0-1 exp is level 1, 2-4 exp is level 2, and 5 exp is level 3. The x on the left will clear the exp bar leaving the pet at level 1 with 0 exp.
  • E: This is a drag and drop tool. Use this to reorder your pets.
  • Simulate: Run the simulation.
  • Randomize: Randomize the Player's and Opponents's pets and equipment.
  • Report A Bug: Report a bug to the SAP Calculator develepor.
  • Export Calculator: Saves a code to your devices clipboard that can be used to import the calculator state.
  • Import Calculator: Import a calculator state.
